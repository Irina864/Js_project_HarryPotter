Hufflepuff is the most inclusive among the four houses, valuing hard work, dedication, patience, loyalty, and fair play rather than a particular aptitude in its members. Students sorted into Hufflepuff often demonstrated strong abilities in Herbology, owing to their correspondence to earth.
The emblematic animal is a badger, and the house colors were yellow and black. The Hufflepuff point hourglass contained yellow diamonds.
The House's patron ghost is the Fat Friar.
